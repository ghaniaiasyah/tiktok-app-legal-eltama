Privacy Policy for Eltama

Effective date: [isi tanggal hari ini]

Eltama is an internal analytics web application used to connect an authorized TikTok account through TikTok Login Kit, retrieve TikTok account analytics data, and store the data in Google Sheets for internal reporting and dashboard purposes.

Information We Collect

When a user connects a TikTok account to Eltama through TikTok Login Kit, Eltama may access authorized TikTok account information and analytics data, including basic account information, profile information, account statistics, and video list data, based on the scopes approved by TikTok and authorized by the user.

How We Use Information

Eltama uses the collected information solely for internal analytics, reporting, performance monitoring, and dashboard visualization purposes. The data is stored in Google Sheets and used only for internal business analysis.

Data Sharing

Eltama does not sell personal data and does not share TikTok account data with unrelated third parties. Data is used only for internal reporting by authorized personnel.

Data Retention

Eltama retains data only as long as necessary for internal reporting and analytics purposes.

User Authorization

Eltama accesses TikTok data only after the user explicitly authorizes the connection through TikTok Login Kit.
